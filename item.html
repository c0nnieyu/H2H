Health

The mission of Casa de Salud is to facilitate and deliver high quality clinical and mental health services for uninsured and underinsured patients, focusing on new immigrants and refugees who encounter barriers to accessing other sources of care. *(source: "https://casadesaludstl.org/about-casa-de-salud")

Services Offered

-Medical care for episodic and chronic conditions

-Limited specialty care including psychiatry, gynecology, dermatology, and others

-Basic screening and prevention services

-Dietetic/nutrition counseling

-Referrals to other health care facilities

-Navigation (accompaniment) to appointments at other health care facilities

-Support with financial assistance for health care

-Home visits for support/education regarding chronic health management

Patient advocacy

*(source: "https://casadesaludstl.org/about-casa-de-salud/programs-services)"
